The Challenge

Schools want to support every student. But the students who need support most are often the least likely to ask for it.

The Problem

Students with autism, ADHD, and anxiety often struggle to communicate how they feel verbally. Traditional check-ins rely on speaking up in class or filling in forms. Many students mask their difficulties until crisis point. Staff only find out something is wrong when it's already escalated.

The Solution

Send Signals gives students a private, instant way to share how each lesson went: green, amber, or red. No typing. No speaking. Just three taps in under 10 seconds. Staff see patterns in real time across lessons, days, and weekends — and can step in early, before small struggles become big problems.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data Controller: Send Signals Ltd, United Kingdom.

1. What We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide our service:

  • Student name and school-assigned identifier (from school Microsoft account)
  • Lesson check-in responses (green/amber/red)
  • Pre-set message selections
  • Timestamps of interactions

2. How We Use It

Data is used solely to support student wellbeing and SEND provision within the student's school. We never sell data, use it for advertising, or share it outside the school's SEND team.

3. Children's Data

We comply with the ICO Age Appropriate Design Code. Data minimisation is a core principle. Students never see other students' data. All data is processed with the child's best interests as the primary consideration.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored on UK-based servers. We use Supabase with Row-Level Security to enforce data isolation at the database level. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

5. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and port your data. Contact us at hello@sendsignals.co.uk to exercise any of these rights.

6. Contact

For any privacy concerns, contact: hello@sendsignals.co.uk
